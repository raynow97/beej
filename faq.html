The product

What's actually in the box?

Two Beej cooling boxer-briefs in your size, three reusable cooling wedges, one silicone hygiene pouch, and a printed booklet (A guide to staying cool). Everything ships in a single recyclable box with no plastic inner.

How do the wedges work?

The wedges contain a phase-change material engineered to hold steady at around 16°C — pleasant against skin, not painfully cold. Once chilled in the freezer, each wedge maintains that temperature for around thirty minutes per session, then needs to go back in.

Will it feel too cold?

No. The first few seconds feel like stepping into an air-conditioned room. After that the sensation is even and pleasant for the full thirty minutes. The wedge is engineered specifically to avoid the painful cold of frozen ice packs.

Can I wear it at my desk?

Yes — most men do. The pocket is hidden under a flap of fabric and there's no visible bulge through trousers.

How often should I use it?

One or two thirty-minute sessions a day is what most men settle into. There's no benefit to wearing it longer than that.

How long do the wedges last?

Each wedge holds its target temperature for about thirty minutes per session, and is good for several hundred freeze-thaw cycles — roughly 12 to 18 months of regular use. Replacements are available as a refill pack.

What is the underwear made of?

A modal-cotton blend (60% modal, 35% cotton, 5% spandex) for the body, with a fine cotton mesh on the inside of the front pocket. Modal breathes better than pure cotton in Indian heat. We deliberately avoided polyester near the testes.

Comfort & safety

Is there any risk to cooling?

The wedge is designed to hold at temperatures that are well above any threshold for skin damage. It is not a freezer-cold ice pack. That said, if you have specific medical conditions affecting the area, please speak to your doctor before using any cooling product.

Can I use it after a vasectomy?

Vasectomy aftercare commonly includes icing the area for several days post-procedure. Many of our early customers came to us specifically for that reason. Beej is far more comfortable and discreet than a frozen pea bag. Always follow your urologist's instructions.

Can I wear it during exercise?

For walks, light cardio, and yoga — yes. For heavy training we suggest using it before or after rather than during, simply for fit.

Will it cause condensation through my clothes?

No. We engineered a vapor barrier into the wedge specifically to prevent condensation from forming on the outside in humid weather. Your underwear and trousers stay dry.

Is Beej a medical device?

No. Beej is a comfort and recovery aid. It does not treat, cure, or prevent any condition. If you have a specific health concern, please consult a doctor.

Sizing & care

How do I pick the right size?

The size guide on the shop page maps waist measurement (in inches) to S/M/L/XL/XXL. If you're between sizes, size up — Beej runs slightly snug to keep the wedge in place. We accept size exchanges within 30 days.

How do I wash it?

Machine wash cold on a gentle cycle, with the wedge removed. Air dry. Do not tumble dry, bleach, or iron the front pocket area.

How do I clean the wedges?

Wipe with a damp cloth as needed. The hygiene pouch keeps them clean in the fridge so this is rarely necessary.

Shipping & returns

Where do you ship?

Across India, free of charge for orders above ₹999. Same-day dispatch for orders placed before 2 PM, two-to-five-day delivery to most pin codes.

Do you ship internationally?

Not yet. We're focused on getting things right in India first. International shipping is on the roadmap for late 2026.

What's the return policy?

30-day comfort guarantee on the underwear (unworn or for size exchange). Wedges are non-returnable for hygiene reasons unless they arrive damaged, in which case we replace them at no charge.

How is it packaged?

Discreetly. The shipping label says "Beej" with no further description. The box is an unbranded brown corrugated mailer.
