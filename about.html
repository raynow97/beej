About Beej

A small Indian brand, making thoughtful things for men.

Beej began with a simple frustration. Indian men's wellness had become a noisy, salesy place — full of gummies, telehealth funnels, and Ayurvedic miracle cures. The product we wanted to buy didn't exist. So we made it.

Why we exist

An honest object, made carefully.

The category around men's bodies in India is dominated by two kinds of brand. The supplement-and-consult D2C, where the answer to every problem is a subscription. And the older trade — the WhatsApp-forwarded miracle cures, the roadside hoardings, the silent shame.

We thought there might be space for a third thing: an actual physical object, built well, sold honestly, written about thoughtfully. Beej is our attempt at that.

What we believe

Four quiet principles.

These are the things we measure ourselves against. If you ever feel Beej has drifted from one of them, the email below works and we read it.

01

The product comes first.

Before brand, before content, before marketing. If the wedge isn't right, nothing else matters. We will spend more on the cooling material than the underwear if that's what it takes.

02

We talk to adults.

No bro humour, no euphemisms, no fairness-cream confidence-makeover language. The men buying this are educated and serious. We write that way.

03

Editorial, not promotional.

We teach before we sell. Our journal will, over time, be a useful resource for men who want to understand their bodies — independent of whether they ever buy a Beej kit.

04

Indian-rooted.

Made in Tirupur and Pune. Designed for the Indian climate, the Indian commute, the Indian summer. The name is Sanskrit. The brand is from here.

How it's made

Three places, twelve months.

Beej is sourced and assembled across three Indian manufacturing clusters. We chose makers who already produce for premium domestic and export brands.

Tirupur

The underwear.

The boxer-briefs are cut and sewn in Tamil Nadu's largest knitwear cluster. A modal-cotton blend, mid-rise, with the custom front pocket built in.

Pune

The wedge.

The phase-change wedges are produced by a vendor that supplies cold-chain logistics for vaccines and surgical recovery. Medical-grade outers; food-safe interiors.

Mumbai

Box & booklet.

Final assembly, packaging, printing, and quality control. Recyclable boxes. No plastic in the inner. Booklet printed on uncoated paper.

A note from the founder

It started with a question nobody answered.

"After a procedure I had in 2024, my urologist told me to ice the area for the first three days. He was clear it would help. He was less clear on what to actually use. A frozen pea bag, in the end. It was awkward. It hurt. It felt unserious."

"I went looking for something that someone — anyone — had designed for this. The international options were pricey, slow to ship, and unsubtle. There was no Indian option. So we built one. The Beej Starter Kit is what I wish I'd had on day one."

— The founder, writing in our first newsletter, January 2026.
