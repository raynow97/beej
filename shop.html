Beej is a cooling underwear system designed for everyday comfort. The boxer-brief is cut from a soft modal-cotton blend that breathes well in Indian heat. A discreet front pocket holds a reusable cooling wedge that stays at a pleasant 14–18°C for around half an hour.

It's built for men who sit a lot, ride two-wheelers, work in heat, train hard, or are recovering from a procedure. It is not a medical device. It is a comfort and recovery aid.

Each Starter Kit ships with:

  • 2 × Beej cooling boxer-briefs (modal-cotton blend, mid-rise)
  • 3 × cooling wedges (phase-change material, ~16°C target)
  • 1 × hygiene freezer pouch (BPA-free silicone)
  • 1 × printed booklet — A guide to staying cool
  • Recyclable, plastic-free packaging

Place a wedge inside the hygiene pouch and put it in your fridge or freezer. After about an hour it's ready to use.

Slide the wedge into the front pocket of your Beej and wear normally. Most men find one or two thirty-minute sessions a day works well — at the desk, after a workout, on the commute home.

After use, return the wedge to the hygiene pouch and back to the fridge. Each wedge is good for hundreds of cycles.

Machine wash cold on a gentle cycle, with the wedge removed. Air dry; do not tumble. Do not bleach. Do not iron the front pocket area.

Wedges can be wiped clean with a damp cloth. Replace if any sign of leakage appears (none expected for at least 12–18 months of regular use).

Why Beej

Five details we sweated.

It's a simple-looking object. The work is in the things you don't see. Here's what makes Beej different from a frozen pea bag, a generic ice pack, or the cheaper cooling boxers on Amazon.

01

Phase-change wedge.

Holds at ~16°C, not 0°C. That's why it doesn't hurt, doesn't go numb, and lasts the full half hour without you noticing.

02

Modal-cotton, not polyester.

"Moisture-wicking" polyester is a common shortcut and it's the wrong fabric for the job. Modal breathes better and runs cooler. Cotton stays cotton.

03

Hygiene pouch included.

Your wedges live in a sealed silicone pouch in the fridge. They never touch food or shelves. A small detail that nobody else does.

04

Anatomy-shaped.

The wedge is moulded to nest, not a flat brick that rides up. It cools more surface and stays where it should.

05

Vapor barrier.

A thin internal film prevents condensation in humid weather. Your underwear stays dry. Worth the engineering.

06

Printed booklet.

An actual booklet ships with each kit. Lifestyle notes, care, and quiet reading on the body. Made in India.
