The brief

A small daily ritual, engineered with care.

The testes sit outside the body for a reason — they like to run a few degrees cooler than the rest of you. Modern life makes that hard. Long commutes, tight clothing, hot Indian afternoons, hours in a desk chair.

Beej is a thirty-minute reset. A wedge from the freezer. A pouch in your underwear. The rest of your day, a little more comfortable.

How it works

Four steps. Then forget about it.

No batteries. No app. No subscription unless you want one. Just an old-fashioned cold pack, redesigned for the part of your body modern life forgot.

01

Unbox.

Your kit ships in a single box: two boxer-briefs, three cooling wedges, a hygiene pouch, and a small booklet.

02

Chill.

Slip the wedges into the hygiene pouch and store in your fridge or freezer. They reach target temperature in about an hour.

03

Insert.

Take a wedge from the pouch and slide it into the front pocket of your Beej. The pocket sits flat and stays hidden.

04

Carry on.

Wear it for around thirty minutes — at your desk, on your commute, after a workout. Twice a day is plenty.

The wedge

Built around a single, careful idea: cool, not cold.

Most ice packs freeze at 0°C. Against skin, that's painful, then numb. We use a phase-change material engineered to hold steady at around 16°C — cold enough to do its job, warm enough to feel pleasant.

The wedge shape nests under the scrotum without shifting. The outer is medical-grade, BPA-free. A vapor barrier prevents condensation in humid weather. Every detail is the answer to a problem with the cheap version.

Designed for

The men we built it for.

Beej is a comfort and recovery aid. If you spend long hours sitting, ride a two-wheeler, train hard, or are recovering from a procedure, this is for you.

01

Long sitters.

Ten hours at a desk, an Uber across the city, a flight at the end of it. Heat builds up. The wedge resets the day.
